HTML Tag Cheat Sheet

Headings

Headings go from h1 to h6 and basically go down in size. You need at least one h1, so keep that in mind.

Paragraphs

Paragraphs are marked with the p, and basically any text you type must go in between the oopening p and the closing p.

Lists

Unordered Lists

Unordered lists are marked with ul and each one has a bullet mark.

Types of Lists:

  • Unordered lists marksed with ul
  • Ordered lists marked with li

Ordered Lists

Ordered lists are marked with ol and are numbered. Each one also has an li mark.
